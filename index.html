Why this matters

Access to clean water improves health, keeps kids in school, and unlocks opportunity.

Health

Safe water reduces waterborne disease and supports long-term wellbeing.

Education

Clean water means fewer missed school days and more time to learn.

Time

Local water points give families hours back each day for work and school.

Dignity

Reliable water restores stability and strengthens communities.

FAQ

How do I start a campus fundraiser?

Create a team page, set a goal, and pick a simple event like a lift-a-thon or bake sale.

Where does the money go?

Funds support clean water projects and ongoing maintenance with transparent reporting.

Can I volunteer without fundraising?

Yes. Help with outreach, event tables, and social posts to grow awareness.
